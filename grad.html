Congreowtulations, graduates!

by Bien Casambre

July 2, 2024

The Cats of UPB community extends its warmest congratulations to the graduates of Batch 2024.

Several Cats of UPB volunteers including Imari Tamayo, Deyniel Cadavona, and Lieca Lagrason climbed the stage last July 24th, marking the high end of their UPB journeys.

‘Hindi namin malilimutan ang inyong mga kabutihan at malasakit sa amin,’ wrote the Cats of UPB community in a Facebook post addressed to the freshly crowned bachelors.

The graduation rites of Batch 2024 took place in the Baguio Convention Center, the original home of Gwen, a young member of the family of UPB campus cats.
